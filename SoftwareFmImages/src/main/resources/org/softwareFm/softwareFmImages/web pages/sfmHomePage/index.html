About us

In this age of on-line social interaction, SoftwareFM also provides insight into what software projects your friends are interacting with and, with your permission allows them to see which projects you are using.

As a crowd sourced database, the more you use and contribute the more useful SoftwareFM will become.

 

Business Model

SoftwareFM is in its infancy and we are committed that the core Eclipse Plug-in will remains a free to use service for individuals. We do, however, have to generate enough cash to cover hosting fees and a handful of developers. We don't like the usual model of advertisements as they are too intrusive and waste valuable screen space.

Sometimes you want to find Individuals looking to sell their skills, added value products, value added resellers Maintenance and Support providers etc and we think there should be a place for these companies to sell their wares.

We will be introducing this area very soon and you make the choice to see them when you are interested. So our main income stream will be through a membership model. The added value services that will become available will be driven by the membership, ensuring that they are the right services.
